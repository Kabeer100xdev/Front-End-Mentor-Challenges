FAQs

What is Frontend Mentor, and how will it help me?

Frontend Mentor offers realistic coding challenges to help developers improve their frontend coding skills with projects in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It's suitable for all levels and ideal for portfolio building.

Is Frontend Mentor free?

Yes, Frontend Mentor offers free and premium coding challenges. Free challenges are great for practice, while premium ones help push your skills even further.

Can I use Frontend Mentor projects in my portfolio?

Absolutely! It’s encouraged. Just make sure to personalize and explain your approach to show off your skills and problem-solving abilities.

How can I get help if I’m stuck on a challenge?

You can ask questions on the Frontend Mentor Discord community or browse solutions submitted by others to learn different approaches.
